Global Initiative Targets Oral Health Disparities: Efforts Aimed at Improving Dental Care Access Worldwide

By Ravi Shankar / 02 January 2020

Dr. Sarah Mitchell, a pioneering dentel surgeon, made history today by performing the world's first-of-its-kind joint replacement surgery. The groundbreaking procedure, which took place at St. Mark's Hospital, marks a significant milestone in orthopedic medicine...

Dr. Mitchell utilized a novel approach combining advanced robotic technology with patient-specific implants to tailor the replacement joint precisely to the individual's anatomy, promising improved outcomes and longevity for patients suffering from debilitating joint conditions. This innovative technique represents a leap forward in orthopedic surgery, offering hope to millions of people worldwide who struggle with joint pain and limited mobility. Dr. Mitchell's expertise and dedication to pushing the boundaries of orthopedic care have paved the way for a new era in joint replacement surgery, promising greater precision, reduced recovery times, and enhanced patient satisfaction.
